Our Journey

From Rings Associate to Good Way International

In 2008, we established Rings Associate in India, focusing on supplying dates and dry fruits. From these modest beginnings, we quickly expanded our reach and began supplying high-quality products across the entire Indian market.

2018 – Expanding to the Source

We leased farms in Jordan and Saudi Arabia, processing premium dates at the source and exporting to multiple countries.

Today

From farms to global shelves, we deliver a curated, traceable, and consistently exceptional date experience.
